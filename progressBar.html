Progress bar

  • Progress trackers indicate a set path that a user follows to complete a specific task
  • The addition of numbers would give at least some sort of indication of progression
  • Keeping the user informed of where they currently are within the process or task at hand will give them a sense of control

Accessibility

The title tag needs to support the progress bar by announcing to screen reader the current progress and position on the journey.

It should read out the product type in this example Car insurance, so that user arrive on step 1 will identify that they are in the correct journey.
