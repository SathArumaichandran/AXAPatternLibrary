Accordions

  • Let users have control over the content by expanding it or deferring it for later and decide what to read and what to ignore
  • If users will want to see all content at once, don't use an accordion control
  • Make the entire header selectable
  • The plus icon is the clearer affordance for menu expansion
  • Place Icons to the Left of Label to reduce users' scanning time
    • The user will have full control of when each section is open or closed
    • You can by default have a section opened
