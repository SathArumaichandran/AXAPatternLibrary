Buttons

Usage

  • Primary buttons are used for the main CTA of the page. It is important to make the distinction clear for the primary task
  • Secondary buttons are used for CTA with less importance

Usability

  • Avoid using multiple primary buttons on a single page. Having more than one main call to action reduces their impact, and leads to decision fatigue
