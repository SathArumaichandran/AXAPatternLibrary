Datepicker

  • A date picker is for selecting a date or dates range. Date picker opens in a modal box form. Users click on the calendar icon or press enter key while the calendar icon is in focus to open the date picker
  • Desktop and tablet (landscape and portrait), months sit next to each other and two months is shown by default, current and the following month. Clicking on the left chevron to navigate to the previous month and the right chevron takes to next month
  • Mobile and phablet, months are stacked down, On top - display the current month and the following months followed down. Swipe up or down to navigate to months
  • Date picker - Upon selection of date, close the date picker and fill in the date input fields
  • Date range picker - Users have to confirm the selection of the start and end date by clicking on "Done" CTA

Accessibility

By default, today's date is in focus in the date picker, arrows are used to navigate dates, space bar or enter to select a date. Keyboard tab is used to navigate elements i.e. "close" link or other elements within the date picker modal.
