  1. 1. Anchor link

    Anchor links can be used on content pages that contain three or more screens of information.

    • Act as a table of contents
    • Provide direct access to content of interest
    • Increase discoverability of and engagement with content that requires a long scroll to discover
    • Need to be differentiated from other links on the page so users understand where they will lead
