Descriptive accordion for form buttons

  • To provide information specific to the form button answers to help the users to understand the complicated answers clearly and avoid selecting the wrong answer
  • Place the arrow to indicate the relationship with the triggered elements

Descriptive accordion will be expanded after selecting the form button.

It comes with an arrow to indicate the relationship with the triggered element.

The arrow direction and position can be changed depending on the context
