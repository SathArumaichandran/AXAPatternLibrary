AXA Pattern Library

Welcome to AXA Pattern Library! This library consists of usability and accessibility guidelines, working code, design documentations for better products and digital experiences, epsecially for the quote and buy journey.

The goals of the design system include improving design consistency, providing full guidance on design, usability, accessibility and development best practices.

Design principles

User- centred

Accessible

AXA brand
Be consistent with the current AXA brand style
